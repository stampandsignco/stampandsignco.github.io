Pricing FAQ

Common questions about our rates and what's included.

What counts as a "notarial act"?
A notarial act is each individual signature, acknowledgment, jurat, or oath that requires the notary's seal and signature. For example, if you have one POA that requires two signatures notarized — that counts as two notarial acts.
Are there any platform or technology fees?
Under Florida law, technology and platform fees may be charged separately from the notarial act fee. Any applicable platform fee will be disclosed clearly before your session begins and is typically included in the session rate above.
How do I pay?
Payment is collected securely at the time of your session. We accept all major credit and debit cards.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
Cancellations or reschedules made more than 2 hours before your session are free of charge. Late cancellations (less than 2 hours) may be subject to a $15 rebooking fee.
Do you offer volume or referral pricing for agents?
Yes! Real estate and insurance agents who refer multiple clients can ask about our partner referral program. Contact us directly to discuss a custom arrangement.
